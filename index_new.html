Better racing through sim-dentistry.

The iRacing rating system can encourage antisocial behavior - divebombs, "punt to pass", "it was netcode" collisions. Win or place at all costs. You, gentle racer, are rewarded for good placement every race - you finish higher in the order, your iRating goes up! At the same time, there are limited rewards for safe racing and superior racecraft.

Let's face it, your iRacing license class is not that important.

Rather than sacrifice Safety Rating (SR) for iRating (IR), the innovation of F499 is to prioritize safety and good racing hygiene.

We want to be better racing drivers, not just drivers with large collections of fake internet points.

We chose SR as our driving metric. Our goal is 4.99 on the path to better racing!
